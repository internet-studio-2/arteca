Introducing Arteca

Arteca is an online portal that supports the creation and dissemination of digital scholarly materials that explore the intersection of arts, humanities, science and technology.
With an expanding collection of over 200 books and three journals, Arteca provides tools for the creation of art and scholarship that cuts across several disciplines of professional practice.

What Does Arteca Offer?

Arteca offers not only a wide range of current and classic resources, but also open access to podcasts, multimedia based materials, grey literature materials, a job board and only textbooks for MOOCs.
Arteca is modeled after CogNet, an online community for cognitive sciences, pioneered by MIT Pess in the late 1990's. CogNet has been a subscription-based content portal and platform since 2000, containint the full text of over 550 books and 6 journals.
